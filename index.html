Guidelines for Participants

Each participant must carry their individual kit and usual camp kit, along with a correct and complete Scout/Guide uniform as per APRO-II/III. They should also bring canvas shoes, a toilet kit, a plate, a mug, a spoon, a water bottle, a torch with batteries, a notebook, a pen, a pencil, knotting ropes, staves, and sketch pens. Additionally, participants should bring costumes and necessary accessories for program and rally events, musical instruments, utensils, lanterns, a first aid kit, a bucket, a spade, a shovel, pegs, a hammer, and a ground sheet etc.
